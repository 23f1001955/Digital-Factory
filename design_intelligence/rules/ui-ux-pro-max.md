# UI-UX Pro Max Landing Rules

## Strategy
- Match landing pattern to product type and audience.
- Primary CTA should be the most prominent element on the page.
- Use social proof (testimonials, reviews) before the final CTA.
- Single CTA focus outperforms multiple competing CTAs.
- Form fields <= 3 for best conversion.

## Recommended Patterns By Goal
- Lead generation: Lead Magnet + Form (minimal fields, clear value prop)
- Product sales: Hero + Features + CTA (feature cards, contrasting CTA)
- High-ticket: Scroll-Triggered Storytelling (narrative increases time-on-page 3x)
- SaaS: Bento Grid Showcase (scannable value props, high density without clutter)
- App: App Store Style (device mockups, screenshots carousel, ratings)

## Color Strategy
- Hero section: Brand primary or vibrant color.
- Feature cards: Light background (#FAFAFA or similar).
- CTA: Contrasting accent color (at least 4.5:1 contrast).
- Testimonials: Light bg, quotes in muted color.
- Comparison tables: Highlight your product row with accent color.

## Conversion Optimization
- Deep CTA placement (not just top — repeat CTA after key sections).
- Social proof before CTA.
- Embedded product demo increases engagement.
- Show annual discount (20-30%) for pricing pages.
- Video in hero: 86% higher engagement.
